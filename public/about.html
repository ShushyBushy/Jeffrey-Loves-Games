About Our Founder





Welcome to Jeffrey Loves Games.



Welcome to Jeffrey Loves Games. Our story begins with the founder, Jeffrey Benne, whose passion for games and unwavering determination led him on an incredible journey from homelessness to creating a thriving gaming company.


Jeffreys life took an unexpected turn when he found himself facing homelessness. Struggling to make ends meet, he sought solace in the world of video games. Gaming became an escape from the harsh realities of his circumstances, providing him with a glimmer of hope and a source of inspiration.

With nothing but his unwavering spirit and a strong desire to create, Jeffrey decided to leverage his love for games and embark on a mission to change his life. He began by collecting discarded electronic devices and salvaging parts to build a modest computer from scratch.


Setting up a small makeshift shack in his backyard, Jeffrey turned this humble space into his gaming haven. Despite the challenges of his circumstances, he dedicated countless hours to honing his skills in game development, teaching himself programming languages and studying game design principles.

Driven by his passion and the desire to share his creations with others, Jeffrey started developing his own games within the confines of his backyard shack. He poured his heart and soul into each project, pushing the boundaries of what was possible with his limited resources.


As Jeffreys reputation grew, so did his ambitions. With the support of his newfound community, he was able to secure a small loan to expand his operation. He relocated his modest shack to a proper workspace and assembled a dedicated team of like-minded individuals who shared his vision.

Together, they poured their collective creativity and expertise into developing games that captured the hearts of gamers around the world. Jeffrey Loves Games quickly became synonymous with innovation, quality, and a unique approach to storytelling within the gaming industry.
