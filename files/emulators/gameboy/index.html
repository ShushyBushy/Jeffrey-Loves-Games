Jeffrey Loves Games - Pollen Boy

A Gameboy Emulator made in JavaScript


This may look complicated, but it's quick to understand.

Step 1: Click any of the game buttons in order to download a ROM (This is essentially a file containing the game)

Step 2: Click the "Choose Rom" button on the gameboy at the bottom. Your downloads folder should open up.

Step 3: Select the ROM file, and that's it! Enjoy playing your classic game!
