FAQ





Here are Jeffrey Loves Games most asked questions:



1. Why did you make this website? I, Jeffrey Benne, created this website because I believe everybody has the right to play fun games. Me myself, I love to play solitaire. It's my favorite game. Whether you're somebody at home, or a student, I hope you have fun on this website.
2. How do I play the games on your website? Our games are designed to be played directly in your web browser, without the need for any installations or downloads. Simply visit our website, choose a game you'd like to play, and click on the game button. The game will load in your browser, and you can start playing right away.
3. How often do you release new games? We strive to release new games regularly to keep our players entertained and engaged. The frequency of game releases may vary, but we are dedicated to providing fresh and exciting content for our community.
4. May I get an autograph from Jeffrey Benne himself? No. No you may not.
